Welcome to Pin ‘Em All

About the Game

A Thrilling Balloon-Popping Adventure!

Dive into the addictive world of Pin-Em-All, the ultimate hyper-casual balloon-shooting game! Put your precision, patience, and timing to the test as you aim, shoot, and burst balloons across 130 unique and exciting levels. Each level offers new challenges to keep you on your toes.

Progress through increasingly tricky levels, hitting special dart and speed-boost balloons to enhance your gameplay. Are you ready to Pin-Em-All and claim your title as the ultimate dart master?

Game Features

  • 130 Unique Levels: New challenges in every level.
  • Precision and Timing: Aim for the perfect shot.
  • Extra Dart Collection: Catch dart balloons to gather more darts.
  • Boosted Dart Speed: Hit speed-up balloons for rapid-fire action.
  • Play Anywhere: Enjoy offline and online gameplay.
  • Progress Saving: Automatically saved every 10 levels.
